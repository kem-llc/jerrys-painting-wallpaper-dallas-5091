Our story

Bringing Color and Quality to Dallas Homes

Our mission is simple: deliver exceptional painting services with integrity, craftsmanship, and a personal touch.

Jerry's Painting & Wallpaper started over a decade ago with a single goal: to provide Dallas homeowners with painting services that exceed expectations. Founded by Jerry himself, a lifelong painter with an eye for detail, the company grew through word-of-mouth and a commitment to quality. From small condos in Uptown to sprawling estates in Highland Park, we've painted it all.

Today, we're proud to be one of Dallas's most trusted painting companies. Our team undergoes continuous training, uses only the best materials, and treats every home like our own. We believe that a fresh coat of paint can transform not just a house, but the way you feel about your home. Let us show you the Jerry's difference.
