Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Painters Since 2003

To deliver exceptional painting and wallpaper services that enhance the beauty and value of every home we touch, with unwavering quality and integrity.

Jerry's Painting & Wallpaper started as a small, family-run operation with a simple mission: provide Dallas homeowners with painting services that stand the test of time. Founder Jerry Martinez, a master painter with over 20 years of experience, built the business on a foundation of hard work, attention to detail, and genuine care for every client. What began as one-man crew has grown into a team of skilled professionals who share Jerry's passion for craftsmanship.

Today, we serve the entire Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex, from historic homes in Highland Park to modern builds in Frisco. We've painted thousands of homes and earned a reputation for reliability, cleanliness, and stunning results. Every project, big or small, receives the same meticulous preparation and high-quality materials. We're proud to be a trusted name in Dallas painting.
