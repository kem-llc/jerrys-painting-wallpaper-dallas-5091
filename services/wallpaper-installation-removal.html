Wallpaper Installation & Removal

Overview

Whether you want to add a bold wallpaper accent or remove outdated paper, we handle it all. Our team has years of experience with all types of wallpaper — from peel-and-stick to traditional paste — and we ensure seamless patterns and clean edges.

What's included

  • Hides wall imperfections
  • Removal service leaves walls smooth and ready

Common questions

Can you remove old wallpaper?

Yes! We use steamers and solvents to remove wallpaper without damaging drywall, then prep the walls for paint or new paper.

How long does wallpaper installation take?

A standard room usually takes 1-2 days, depending on pattern matching and complexity.
