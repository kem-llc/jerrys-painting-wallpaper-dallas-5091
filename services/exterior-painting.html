Overview

Our exterior painting service revitalizes your home's curb appeal while providing a protective barrier against Dallas sun, rain, and wind.

What's included

  • Enhance curb appeal and property value
  • Protect against weather and moisture damage
  • Long-lasting, fade-resistant finishes
  • Comprehensive surface prep for maximum adhesion

How it works

1

Inspection & Power Wash

We inspect surfaces for damage and power wash to remove dirt and old paint.

2

Repairs & Caulking

We repair wood rot, replace damaged siding, and caulk gaps to seal out moisture.

3

Primer & Paint

We apply high-quality primer and two coats of exterior paint for a durable, beautiful finish.

Common questions

What type of paint do you use for exteriors?

How long will my exterior paint job last?

With proper preparation and quality paint, expect 5-10 years before needing a refresh.
