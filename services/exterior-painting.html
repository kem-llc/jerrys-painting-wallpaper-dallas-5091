Exterior Painting

Protect and beautify your home's exterior with weather-resistant paints.

Schedule this service

Overview

Our exterior painting service includes thorough preparation — pressure washing, scraping, caulking, and priming — followed by two coats of high-quality exterior paint. We work efficiently to minimize disruption and ensure a stunning, long-lasting finish.

What's included

  • Boosts curb appeal and home value
  • Protects against sun, rain, and pests
  • Prevents wood rot and siding damage

How it works

1

Inspection & Prep

We inspect the exterior, pressure wash, scrape loose paint, and repair any damage.

2

Caulking & Priming

We caulk gaps, seal windows and doors, and apply primer to bare wood or metal.

3

Painting & Final Walkthrough

We apply two coats of premium exterior paint and review the work with you to ensure your satisfaction.

Common questions

How long does exterior paint last?

With our high-quality paints and proper prep, expect 5-10 years depending on climate and surface.

Do you paint in winter?

We paint year-round as long as temperatures are above 40°F. We'll schedule around weather for best results.
